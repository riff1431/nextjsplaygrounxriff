APPEALS POLICY
1. Right to Appeal

Users may appeal:

content removals
account suspensions
payout holds
2. Submission

Appeals must include:

account details
reason for appeal
supporting evidence

Submit to: support@playgroundx.vip

3. Review Process
reviewed by a separate moderation reviewer where possible
evaluated against platform policies
decision provided within reasonable timeframe
4. Final Decisions

PlayGroundX decisions are final after appeal.

We aim to respond to appeals within 7–14 days.

If you are not satisfied, you may escalate the matter or pursue dispute resolution as permitted under applicable law, including under EU Digital Services Act (DSA) provisions where applicable.
