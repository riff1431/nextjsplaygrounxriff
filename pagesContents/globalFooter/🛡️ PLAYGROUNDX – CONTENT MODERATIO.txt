Content is strictly prohibited if it includes:

🔴 Illegal Content
minors (under 18)
exploitation or abuse
non-consensual acts
human trafficking
terrorism-related material
🔴 Non-Compliant Adult Content
lack of consent
extreme violence or harm
prohibited acts (incest, bestiality, etc.)
🔴 Harmful Conduct
harassment, doxxing, threats
hate speech or discrimination
🔴 Platform Abuse
fraud or scams
payment circumvention

🔹 Automated Tools

🔹 User Reporting
real-time reporting tools
complaint submissions
5. NOTICE & ACTION (DSA REQUIREMENT)

Users may report content they believe is illegal or violates our policies.

📩 How to Report

Users can:

click “Report” on content
email: support@playgroundx.vip

Reports should include:

URL or username
description of issue
supporting evidence (if available)
6. REVIEW PROCESS

Upon receiving a report, we will:

review the content
assess legality and policy compliance
request additional information if needed
take appropriate action
⏱️ Timeframe
Typically reviewed within 24–72 hours
Faster for high-risk content
7. POSSIBLE ACTIONS

We may:

remove content
restrict visibility
suspend or terminate accounts
report to law enforcement
8. STATEMENT OF REASONS (DSA REQUIREMENT)

If we take action (e.g. remove content or suspend account), we will:

notify the affected user
provide a statement of reasons, including:
what rule was violated
what action was taken

⏱️ Timeline
