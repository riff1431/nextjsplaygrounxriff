Creators must ensure that all content:

🚫 PROHIBITED CONTENT

Creators may NOT:

Creators must:

behave professionally within Rooms

5. MONETIZATION STRUCTURE

Creators earn based on Room type and interactions.

💰 Public Rooms
Entry Fee: 0% Creator / 100% Platform
Per-Minute Billing: 0% Creator / 100% Platform
Tips: 85% Creator / 15% Platform (default)
Gifts: up to 100% Creator
💰 Private Rooms
Entry Fee: 40% Creator / 60% Platform
Per-Minute Billing: 40% Creator / 60% Platform
Tips: 85% Creator / 15% Platform (default)
Gifts: up to 100% Creator
6. TIPS, GIFTS & INTERACTIONS

Gifts may carry different payout structures

⚠️ IMPORTANT:
PlayGroundX may modify:

tip splits
