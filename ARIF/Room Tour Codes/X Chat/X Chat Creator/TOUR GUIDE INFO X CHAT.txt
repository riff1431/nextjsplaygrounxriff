# X CHAT CREATOR ROOM TOUR

## STEP 1 — LIVE CHAT PANEL

### Title:

Live Chat Panel

### Description:

View and manage all fan messages in real-time.

### What To Do:

Switch between:

• All Messages
• Paid Messages
• Priority Messages

Use this area to communicate directly with your fans and keep conversations active throughout your session.

---

## STEP 2 — INCOMING REQUESTS

### Title:

Incoming Requests

### Description:

View requests submitted by fans.

### What To Do:

• Review incoming requests.
• Open request details.
• Respond to fan requests.
• Prioritize paid requests.
• Keep fans engaged with quick responses.

All fan requests will appear here during your session.

---

## STEP 3 — SUMMARY STATS

### Title:

Summary Stats

### Description:

Track your session performance in real-time.

### What To Monitor:

• Reactions
• Stickers
• Paid Messages
• Live Fans
• Requests

Use these statistics to measure engagement and performance during your live session.

---

## STEP 4 — EARNED AMOUNT

### Title:

Earned Amount

### Description:

Track how much you have earned during the current session.

### What To Do:

• Monitor earnings in real-time.
• Track performance during the session.
• Measure the effectiveness of fan engagement.

Your earnings update as fans interact and make purchases.

---

## STEP 5 — LIVE FANS

### Title:

Live Fans

### Description:

See how many fans are currently watching your session.

### What To Do:

• Monitor audience growth.
• Track fan activity.
• Measure engagement levels.

A growing fan count usually means more interaction opportunities.

---

## STEP 6 — TIMER & CONTROL

### Title:

Timer & Control

### Description:

Start and manage your X Chat session.

### What To Do:

• Click the green button when you are ready to go live.
• Start your X Chat session.
• Monitor your session duration.
• End the session when finished.

The timer begins once your live session starts.
