# FLASH DROP CREATOR ROOM TOUR

## STEP 1 — SUMMARY BOX

### Title:

Summary Box

### Description:

Track your room performance in real-time.

### What To Do:

Monitor:

* Total Fans
* Active Drops
* High Roller Packs
* Custom Requests
* Total Tips

Use these metrics to understand what content is performing best and where your earnings are coming from.

## STEP 2 — LIVE DROP BOARD

### Title:

Live Drop Board

### Description:

Create and manage content drops for your fans.

### What To Do:

* Add Photo Drops.
* Add Video Drops.
* Launch New Content.
* Monitor Active Drops.
* Track Sales Performance.
* Create urgency with limited-time drops.

Your Live Drop Board is your primary content marketplace inside the room.

---

## STEP 3 — LIVE STREAM

### Title:

Live Stream

### Description:

Broadcast live and engage directly with your audience.

### What To Do:

* Welcome new fans.
* Promote active drops.
* Preview upcoming content.
* Interact with viewers.
* Encourage purchases and tips.

Fans are more likely to purchase content when you're actively engaging with them live.

## STEP 4 — HIGH ROLLER PACKS

### Title:

High Roller Packs

High Roller Packs are designed for your highest-spending fans.

## STEP 5 — CUSTOM REQUEST DROPS

### Title:

Custom Request Drops

### Description:

Manage personalized content requests submitted by fans.

### What To Do:

Review:

* Requested Content
* Custom Instructions

* Decline Requests

Custom requests often generate some of the highest-value sales inside the room.

## STEP 6 — LIVE CHAT

### Title:

Live Chat

### Description:

Interact with fans in real-time.
