### Title:

Creator Info

### Description:

View the creator’s profile picture and connection status.

### What To Do:

* Check which creator is hosting the room.
* View the creator’s name and profile image.
* See if the creator is currently connected.
* Tap the creator profile area to learn more when available.

## STEP 2 — QUICK ACTIONS

### Title:

Quick Actions

### Description:

Use the side buttons to quickly interact with the room.

### What To Do:

Use these buttons to:

* Go back.
* Follow or add the creator.
* Share the room.
* Report any issues.
* View incoming content sent from the creator.

These buttons help you manage your fan experience quickly.

## STEP 3 — LIVE DROP BOARD

### Title:

Live Drop Board

### Description:

View content drops available from the creator.

### What To Do:

* See active drops.
* Browse all drops.
* Filter by Photos.
* Filter by Videos.
* Watch for new drops from the creator.
* Purchase content when available.

Stay ready because drops may be limited or available for a short time.

### Title:

Live Chat

### Description:

Chat in real-time with the creator and other fans in the room.

* Ask questions.

Live chat helps you join the room experience instead of just watching.

## STEP 7 — REQUEST A DROP

### Title:

Request a Drop

### Description:

Request custom content directly from the creator.

### What To Do:

* Describe what you want to see.

Be clear and respectful so the creator understands your request.
