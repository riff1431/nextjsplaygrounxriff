CONFESSION ROOM FAN TOUR

STEP 1 — 1 ON 1 & GLOBAL

Title:
1 on 1 & Global

Description:
Choose where your confession request goes.

What To Do:

* Select 1 on 1 to send your confession request directly to the creator in this room.
* Select Global to make your confession request visible to all available creators.
* Use 1 on 1 when you want this specific creator to respond.
* Use Global when you want more creators to see and possibly respond to your request.

---

STEP 2 — ALL CREATOR CONFESSIONS

Title:
All Creator Confessions

Description:
Browse confessions created by creators.

What To Do:

* View confession content created by the creator.
* Use filters to find confessions by mood, interest, or category.
* Filter by categories such as:

  * All
  * Spicy
  * Dirty
  * Bedroom
  * Forbidden
* Use gift-style filters such as:

  * Kiss
  * Love
  * Spicy
  * Diamond

---

STEP 3 — REQUEST CONFESSION

Title:
Request Confession

Description:
Create a confession request for the creator.

What To Do:

* Choose your request type:

  * Text
  * Image
  * Video
* Enter your offer amount.
* Add a topic prompt to guide the creator.
* Make the request clear so the creator understands what you want.

---

STEP 4 — INVITE / INCOMING

Title:
Invite / Incoming

Description:
Invite friends and view incoming room activity.

What To Do:

* Use Invite to bring friends into the room.
* Use Incoming to view:

  * Notifications
  * Creator responses
  * Request updates
  * Room activity

---

STEP 5 — LIVE CHAT

Title:
Live Chat

Description:
Chat with the creator and other fans in real-time.

What To Do:

* Send messages.
* React to confessions.
* Join the conversation.
* Ask questions.
* Stay active and respectful.

---

STEP 6 — CREATOR SPOTLIGHT

Title:
Creator Spotlight

Description:
View the creator currently hosting the room.

What To Do:

* See which creator is live.
* View the creator’s profile area.
* Check live status.
* Stay updated on their current activity and connection status.

---

STEP 7 — SEND REQUEST

Title:
Send Request

Description:
Submit your confession request.

What To Do:

* Review your request before sending.
* Confirm your offer amount.
* Check whether the request is 1 on 1 or Global.
* Choose whether it should be public or anonymous.
* Click Send Request to submit it.

---

STEP 8 — MY REQUESTS LIST

Title:
My Requests List

Description:
Track confession requests you have submitted.

What To Do:

* View your active requests.
* Check request status.
* Track creator responses.
* See whether your request is pending, accepted, rejected, or completed.

CONFESSION ROOM FAN SUCCESS FLOW

1. Enter the Confession Room.

2. Check the Creator Spotlight.

3. Browse All Creator Confessions.

4. Join Live Chat.

5. Choose Text, Image, or Video request.

6. Enter your offer amount.

7. Add a clear topic prompt.

8. Choose Public or Anonymous.

9. Choose 1 on 1 or Global.

10. Click Send Request.

11. Track your request in My Requests List.

12. Stay active in chat while waiting for a response.

---

PRO TIPS

✔ Use 1 on 1 when you want the current creator specifically.

✔ Use Global if you want more creators to see your confession request.

✔ Add a clear topic prompt.

✔ Higher offer amounts may get more attention.

✔ Use Anonymous if you want privacy.

✔ Stay active in Live Chat.

✔ Browse creator confessions before sending a request.

✔ Track your request status in My Requests List.

The more clearly you write your confession request, the easier it is for the creator to respond properly.
