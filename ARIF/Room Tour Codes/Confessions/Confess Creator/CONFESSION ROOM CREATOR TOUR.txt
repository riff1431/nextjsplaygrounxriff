CONFESSION ROOM CREATOR TOUR

STEP 1 — MY REQUESTS

Title:
My Requests

Description:
View confession requests submitted directly to you by fans.

What To Do:

* Review incoming confessions.
* Read fan submissions.
* Accept or reject requests.
* Respond to confessions that interest you.
* Prioritize higher value submissions when appropriate.

---

STEP 2 — GLOBAL REQUESTS

Title:
Global Requests

Description:
Browse public confession requests submitted by all users.

What To Do:

* Discover trending confessions.
* Find interesting topics to discuss.
* Select confessions you want to react to live.
* Use popular confessions to increase engagement.

---

STEP 3 — PENDING REQUESTS

Title:
Pending Requests

Description:
Displays confession requests waiting for your action.

What To Do:
Review:

* Fan Name
* Confession
* Amount Offered

Choose:

* Accept
* Decline
* Respond

Higher value requests may generate additional earnings and engagement.

---

STEP 4 — EARNINGS & LIVE FANS

Title:
Earnings & Live Fans

Description:
Track your room performance in real-time.

What To Do:
Monitor:

* Current Earnings
* Live Fan Count

Use these metrics to understand which confessions generate the most interest and participation.

---

STEP 5 — START / END ROOM

Title:
Start / End Room

Description:
Controls your live Confession Room session.

What To Do:
When ready:

* Click Start Room.
* Welcome fans.
* Begin reviewing confessions.

When finished:

* Thank your audience.
* Encourage follows and subscriptions.
* End the room professionally.

---

STEP 6 — CREATE RANDOM CONFESSION

Title:
Create Random Confession

Description:
Create a random confession to display publicly on the Fan view.

What To Do:

* Generate discussion topics.
* Keep the room active during slower periods.
* Encourage fan participation.
* Spark new conversations.

Random confessions help maintain room activity and engagement.

---

STEP 7 — CONFESSION WALL

Title:
Confession Wall

Description:
Manage confessions displayed publicly within your room.

What To Do:

* Highlight interesting confessions.
* Showcase popular submissions.
* Create discussion around trending topics.
* Encourage fans to submit their own confessions.

Popular confessions often lead to increased engagement and tips.

---

STEP 8 — SUMMARY PANEL

Title:
Summary Panel

Description:
Provides an overview of room performance.

Displays:

* Total Fans
* Total Confessions
* Reaction Tips
* Total Earnings

What To Do:
Use these statistics to measure room success and identify which content performs best.

---

STEP 9 — LIVE CHAT

Title:
Live Chat

Description:
Interact with fans in real-time.

What To Do:

* Welcome new fans.
* Ask questions.
* React to confessions.
* Encourage participation.
* Build discussions around submitted confessions.

The more active the chat, the longer fans typically stay in the room.

---

CONFESSION ROOM SUCCESS FLOW

1. Start Room.

2. Welcome fans.

3. Review My Requests.

4. Browse Global Requests for interesting topics.

5. Accept valuable Pending Requests.

6. Discuss confessions live.

7. Generate Random Confessions when activity slows.

8. Use the Confession Wall to showcase popular content.

9. Keep Live Chat active.

10. Encourage confession submissions.

11. Collect tips and reactions.

12. End session professionally.

---

PRO TIPS

✔ React naturally to confessions.

✔ Ask follow-up questions.

✔ Encourage fans to share their thoughts.

✔ Use humor and personality.

✔ Feature popular confessions on the Confession Wall.

✔ Keep discussions moving.

✔ Thank fans for tips and submissions.

✔ Encourage subscriptions before ending the session.

The more engaging your reactions and discussions are, the longer fans stay and the more opportunities you create for tips, reactions, and repeat visits.
