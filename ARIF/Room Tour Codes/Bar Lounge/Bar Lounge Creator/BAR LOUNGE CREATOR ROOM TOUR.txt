BAR LOUNGE CREATOR ROOM TOUR

STEP 1 — ROOM INFO

Title:
Room Info

Description:
Shows your current earnings only.

What To Do:
Keep an eye on this number throughout your stream to monitor your performance and income generated from fans.

---

STEP 2 — LOUNGE CHAT

Title:
Lounge Chat

Description:
This is your main chat with fans.

What To Do:

* Welcome new fans as they enter.
* Answer questions.
* Build conversations.
* Encourage tips and virtual drinks.
* Create a fun and engaging atmosphere.
* Keep fans entertained so they stay longer.

---

STEP 3 — INCOMING REQUESTS

Title:
Incoming Requests

Description:
Click here to see and manage fan requests.

What To Do:
Fans can request:

* VIP Table access
* Private sessions
* Special interactions

Review each request and decide whether to:

* Accept
* Decline
* Respond with a message

---

STEP 4 — ROOM CONTROLS

Title:
Room Controls

Description:
Access your room management tools.

What To Do:
Use these controls to:

* Enable or disable camera
* Enable or disable microphone
* Manage settings
* Ban users
* Block users
* Clear chat
* Control moderation tools

These controls help you manage your room professionally.

---

STEP 5 — START / END ROOM

Title:
Start / End Room

Description:
Control your live session.

What To Do:
When ready:

* Click Start Room to go live.
* Begin greeting fans immediately.
* Keep conversation active.

When finished:

* Click End Room.
* Thank fans for attending.
* Encourage them to follow and subscribe before leaving.

---

STEP 6 — ROOM TIMER

Title:
Room Timer

Description:
Tracks how long you have been live.

What To Do:
Use the timer to:

* Monitor session length.
* Schedule breaks.
* Manage your streaming sessions effectively.

Longer engaging sessions generally create more earning opportunities.

STEP 8 — INCOMING REQUESTS SECTION

Title:
Incoming Requests Section

Description:
Review and manage fan requests here.

What To Do:
When a request arrives:

Review:

* Request type
