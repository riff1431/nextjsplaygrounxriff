TRUTH OR DARE — CREATOR ROOM TOUR GUIDE

STEP 1 — LIVE STREAM PREVIEW

Description:
See your live stream preview in real-time.

What To Do:

• Start your stream.
• Monitor your live status.
• Check how many fans are watching.
• Engage with viewers once live.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 2 — INVITE CREATOR SLOTS

Description:
Invite co-creators to join your Truth or Dare room.

What To Do:

• Click the "+" icon.
• Invite creators into the room.
• Host group Truth or Dare sessions.
• Up to 4 co-creators can join.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 3 — ROOM EARNINGS

Description:
Track your earnings during the session.

What To Do:

Monitor:

• Total Earned
• Tips
• Truth Earnings
• Dare Earnings
• Custom Request Earnings

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 4 — GROUP VOTE

Description:
Allow fans to vote between Truth or Dare.

What To Do:

• Select Truth or Dare.
• Add a prompt.
• Set votes required.
• Set vote duration.
• Set how much fans pay per vote.
• Click Start Vote.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 5 — INCOMING REQUESTS

Description:
Manage incoming fan requests.

What To Do:

• Review Truth requests.
• Review Dare requests.
• Review Custom requests.
• Accept requests.
• Reject requests.
• Complete approved requests live.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 6 — LIVE CHAT

Description:
Communicate with your audience in real-time.

What To Do:

• View fan messages.
• Reply to fans.
• Encourage participation.
• Encourage votes.
• Encourage tips.
• Build stronger fan relationships.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 7 — ROOM TIMER

Description:
Shows how long your session has been running.

What To Do:

• Monitor session length.
• Track active room time.
• Manage your session flow.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 8 — GO LIVE / END SESSION

Description:
Start or end your Truth or Dare room.

What To Do:

• Click Go Live to start the room.
• Once live, click End to finish the session.
• Ending the session closes the room and stops all live activity.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

TRUTH OR DARE SUCCESS FLOW

1. Click Go Live.

2. Welcome fans into the room.

3. Invite creators if desired.

4. Launch your first Group Vote.

5. Encourage fans to vote.

6. Complete Truths and Dares live.

7. Review Incoming Requests.

8. Engage continuously in Live Chat.

9. Encourage tips and participation.

10. Monitor your earnings.

11. Keep the room entertaining and active.

12. End the session professionally.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PRO TIPS

✔ Keep votes short and exciting.

✔ Use creative Truth prompts.

✔ Create entertaining Dare challenges.

✔ Thank fans publicly for tips.

✔ Encourage custom requests.

✔ Keep Live Chat active.

✔ Invite guest creators for bigger audiences.

✔ Reward top supporters.

✔ Build excitement around every vote.

✔ End each session by teasing your next live event.

The more interaction, voting, requests, participation, and engagement you generate, the higher your earnings, fan retention, and room growth will become.
